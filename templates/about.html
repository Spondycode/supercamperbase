Who Am I?
David Allen

I live on the Costa Brava, Catalonia, Spain.

After working as a receptionist at a campsite by the beach in Platja d'Aro for 15 years, I claim to have some idea of what campers are looking for from a plot. Some people have to have a plot which allows use of the satellite TV and nothing else matters. During the hottest summer days it can be vital to having a good holiday to be under the trees and benefitting from shade from the blazing sun. Cooler months the opposite would be true and the campers coming south for the hot weather like to sweat it out as much as possible.

My idea is for the site to be a place where campers can share their experiences of the plots they have stayed on. I hope that the site will be useful to campers and campsite owners alike. Campers will be able to find the best plots and campsite owners will be able to see what their customers think of their plots. I don't want this site to be a place for people to complain about the campsite owners. I want it to be a place where campers can share their experiences and help each other to find the best plots.

As the site grows I want to offer interactivity where you can keep in contact with other campers. You will be able to make comments on other campers' reviews and ask questions. In time we will have items you can buy as souvenirs of your camping trips. I am thinking of mugs, t-shirts and stickers. I am open to suggestions for other items you would like to see.

I am also open to suggestions for other features you would like to see on the site. I am also thinking of a section where you can share your camping recipes. I am open to suggestions for other features you would like to see.If there are folks who want to write an article about their camping experiences I would be happy to publish them on the site. Let's see how it goes.
