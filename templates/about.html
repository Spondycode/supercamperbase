Who Am I?
David Allen

I live on the Costa Brava, Catalonia, Spain.

After working as a receptionist at a campsite by the beach in Platja d'Aro, Catalonia, Spain for 15 years, I can claim to have some idea of what campers are looking for from a plot. Some people have to have a plot which allows use of the satellite TV and nothing else matters. During the hottest summer days it can be vital to having a good holiday to get a plot under the trees and benefit from shade from the blazing sun. Cooler months the opposite would be true. Campers coming south from the cold North for the hot weather in Spain like to sweat it out as much as possible. Everyone's different. Then there are those that just need a spot close to the toilets and showers. Not much fun having to walk to the other side of the campsite in the middle of the night, when you get the call to nature.

Let's be Nice...

My idea is for the site to be a place where SuperCampers can share your experiences of the plots they have stayed on. I hope that the site will be useful to campers and campsite owners alike. Campers will be able to find the best plots and campsite owners will be able to see what their customers think of their plots. I don't want this site to be a place for people to complain about the campsite owners. If you want to have a moan then go do it on a site like Google or on a social network. I want SuperCamper to be a place where you can share your experiences and help each other to find the best plots.

More to Come

Already you can leave comments on plot postings as well as replies to the comments. Add a like to the plot or to the comment. As the site grows we will offer more interactivity where you can keep in contact with other campers. You will be able to connect with other campers' reviews and ask questions. In time we will have items you can buy as souvenirs of your camping trips. Buy SuperCamper mugs, t-shirts and stickers and useful camping tools. SuperCamper is open to suggestions for other items you would like to see here on the site.

What Else Would You like to See Here on SuperCamper

I am also open to suggestions for other features you would like to see on the site. I am also thinking of a section where you can share your camping recipes. I am open to suggestions for other features you would like to see.If there are folks who want to write an article about their camping experiences I would be happy to publish them on the site. Let's see how it goes.
